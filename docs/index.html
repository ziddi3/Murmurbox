What is ShineChain?

Dimensional Mythology

ShineChain is an art-science mythology that explores the hidden dimensions of our reality. It interprets the invisible world around us through technology and narrative, creating a bridge between science and storytelling.

Sensory Exploration

Using specialized sensors that detect ultraviolet light, infrared radiation, magnetic fluctuations, and barometric pressure changes, ShineChain artifacts reveal patterns and anomalies that exist beyond human perception.

AI Interpretation

Through the power of local AI models, these invisible patterns are transformed into mythological narratives — "transmissions" from entities that exist in parallel dimensions, creating a new form of technological storytelling.

The Murmur Box: Dimensional Tuner

The primary artifact of the ShineChain Experience is the Murmur Box — a device that listens to the invisible world and translates its murmurs into meaningful transmissions.

Multi-Sensor Fusion

The Murmur Box integrates multiple sensors to detect phenomena across different spectra: UV/IR light (camera + UVA/UVB), magnetic flux (LIS3MDL), barometric pressure (BMP280), optional EMF coil/ADC, and optional microphone.

Lore Engine

At its heart, the Murmur Box contains a Lore Engine powered by a local gpt-oss model. This engine transforms sensor anomalies into ShineChain "codex entries" — mythological interpretations of real-world phenomena.

Physical Artifacts

Each transmission is materialized through multiple outputs: spoken word via audio, visual display on OLED screens, and permanent records via thermal printer — creating tangible evidence of the invisible world.

The ShineChain Experience

Experiencing ShineChain is a journey through the unseen dimensions that surround us every day. Here's what happens during a typical ShineChain session:

Preparation

The Murmur Box is placed in a space of interest — this could be a room with reported anomalies, a natural setting with unique energy patterns, or simply a space you wish to explore dimensionally.

Sensing

For 10-30 seconds, the Box's sensors actively poll the environment, measuring variations in ultraviolet light, infrared radiation, magnetic fields, and barometric pressure. The system looks for variances, spikes, and cross-sensor coincidences.

Observation

The raw sensor data is processed into a compact JSON "Observation" that summarizes the detected phenomena — for example, a UV spike coinciding with magnetometer jitter and a low-frequency pressure drift.

Mythologizing

The Observation is fed into the Lore Engine along with fragments of ShineChain canon. The local AI model interprets these patterns through the lens of ShineChain mythology, creating a narrative explanation.

Transmission

A complete codex page is generated, including a title, transmission text, and structured metadata. This transmission might reveal messages from entities like "Thread-42" or "The Obsidian Tear" — mythological constructs that represent different aspects of dimensional phenomena.

Embodiment

The transmission manifests physically: spoken aloud through audio synthesis, displayed on an OLED screen, and printed on thermal paper as a permanent record. Each artifact becomes a tangible connection to the invisible world.
